  • Psyduck

    Psyduck is just a regular guy trying to make it in the world. In that way, he isn't so different from you and me. Like any regular guy, he puts his pants on one leg at a time. Proverbial pants, of course. I'm not going to say he's the toughest dude out there because he's not. Does he complain about his headaches? Sure, sometimes. Would you if you had headaches all day? Probably.

    Next time you see a Psyduck on the street, you don't need to salute him or shake his hand. He's not a hero, and he's not pretending to be one. Just greet him with a strong handhshake and look him in the eye when he's talking to you. He'll treat you in kind.

  • Pikachu

    Pikachu is a cold-blooded killer, a mercenary that never spent a paycheck on anything but more bullets nor a splinter of attention on anything but his next target. He has an elite martial arts training and his marksmanship with any weapon is unmatched. Having trained with SEAL Team Six, he can operate on both land and sea with full ability. His unique intelligence has allowed him to master the surfboard, making him the perfect agent to handle high profile targets who exclusively stay on beaches with moderate to perfect surfing conditions.

    Pikachu has said that in another life, he would have enjoyed having a succulent garden in Orange County, California.
